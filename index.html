About Us

We are one of the few remaining full-service automotive car repair facilities in the Vicksburg area. Since 1981, Clark's Auto Shop has continually grown and improved to better serve and repair cars for our Vicksburg clients. Our desire is to cultivate a continued relationship with you, your family, and your friends, meeting all your vehicle and car repair needs. We desire to create more than just a satisfied customer; we desire to create loyal clients who cannot imagine a world without Clark's Auto Shop. Simply put, we want you to visit more than once, and we want you to tell everyone you know about your experience with us. We provide the following services to our customers: Car Care Services, Brake Systems, Cool/Heat Systems, Exhaust Systems, Maintenance Service, Electrical Systems, Fuel Systems, Suspension Systems, Drive Train System, Emissions System and more!
