Transmission

What is a transmission? It’s an intricate component of gears and sensors that acts as a bridge between your engine and your wheels. The transmission ensures that the energy provided by the engine travels smoothly to the tires, providing better, safer control for the driver. This activity generates a lot of heat, though. Transmission fluid keeps your transmission from getting too hot and sustaining damage. As this fluid is used over time, it becomes less efficient, leaving your transmission more vulnerable to heat damage. We offer the removal and replacement of your transmission fluid to keep your transmission running strong.

Engine Rebuild/Repair

One of the most important parts of our car is the engine. The car’s engine is able to take burning gas and use that as energy to move the wheel that help drive the car. Without the engine, we are unable to drive our car to work, school, or places that we need to go. When regular maintenance is throw out the window, the engine starts to develop issues. We take old engines, disassemble them, and repair every part inside of it to have your car running like you just pulled it off the showroom floor.

Full Auto Maintenance

The engine of a car can be used and ignored, but that's not in your best interest. That's because your engine requires a lot of maintenance to keep it running right, and part of that maintenance is regularly replacing fluids. We specialize in the removal and replacement of every fluid in your engine, including oil and coolant.
Another service that we provide, and one you will eventually need, is the replacement of your filters that your engine uses to intake air, and the filter for oil.
